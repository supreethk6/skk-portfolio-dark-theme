I’m a senior robotics engineer from San Francisco bay area.

I am passionate about building excellent robots and software that improves the lives

As a Senior Robotics Software Engineer with over half a decade of experience, I specialize in the design and implementation of advanced robotics systems and algorithms that meet client requirements and constraints. My expertise includes robot system architectures, algorithm design, and software implementation, with a proven track record in prototyping, experimentation, and production-level software development for Autonomous Data Machines. I am experienced in leading multiple teams of robotics engineers and collaborating effectively across the organization to optimize the capabilities, efficiency, and robustness of our client services. My passion is to utilize technology to create safer, more engaged communities while reducing costs and crime.

In addition to my experience, I possess a diverse set of skills that support my ability to design, develop, and maintain advanced robotics systems. My technical proficiency includes programming languages such as C++, Python and C, as well as experience with software development tools such as ROS, Git, Linux, OpenCV, CAD, Autoware, Gazebo and MATLAB. My expertise in robotics includes areas such as navigation, mapping, localization, perception, motion planning, kinematics, state machine and controls. I am also familiar with machine learning techniques, including deep learning and computer vision, which I have applied to enhance the capabilities of autonomous systems. My passion for advanced robotics, autonomous driving vehicles, and construction robotics, along with my research experience as a Graduate Research Assistant of Automation and Intelligence for Civil Engineers (AI4CE) Lab at NYU, and my industrial experience at Knightscope, Apsis Solutions, TATA Consultancy Services, and Columbus AI, allows me to bring a unique perspective and innovative solutions to any project.
